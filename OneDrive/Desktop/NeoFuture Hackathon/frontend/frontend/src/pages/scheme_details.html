PM Street Vendor's
AtmaNirbhar Nidhi

Active Scheme timer Expiring in 12 Days

Description

The PM SVANidhi scheme is a central sector scheme launched by the Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs to provide micro-credit facilities to street vendors whose livelihoods were impacted by the pandemic. It empowers urban entrepreneurs with working capital to restart their businesses without any collateral.

The scheme targets vendors in urban areas, including those from surrounding peri-urban and rural areas who have been vending in the urban geographical limits. Beyond credit, it incentivizes digital transactions through monthly cashback and encourages timely repayment with interest subsidies.

Benefits

currency_rupee

Micro-Credit Loan

Initial working capital loan up to ₹10,000, repayable in monthly installments over a year.

trending_down

Interest Subsidy

7% interest subsidy on timely/early repayment, credited directly to the vendor's bank account.

account_balance_wallet

Digital Rewards

Monthly cashback ranging from ₹50 to ₹100 for vendors adopting digital payment methods.

workspace_premium

Loan Graduation

Eligibility for a higher loan of ₹20,000 and ₹50,000 on successful repayment of previous tranches.

Eligibility Criteria

  • check
    Possession of Certificate of Vending / ID Card issued by Urban Local Bodies (ULBs).
  • check
    Vendors identified in the survey but not issued certificates.
  • check
    Street Vendors of surrounding development/peri-urban/rural areas vending in the geographical limits of the ULBs.
  • check
    Vendors who have started vending after the survey completion and possess a Letter of Recommendation (LoR).
